Omelette

Description

This omelette is a breakfast staple that you must incorporate into your day. Eggs are a great source of protein and it is a fast recipe to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice of ham
  • 1/8 cup of cheddar cheese
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 slice of toast (if you choose)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Heat up your pan on medium heat
  2. Whisk egg for 15 seconds
  3. Place butter on pan and spread it as it melts
  4. Place egg on pan and let it cook
  5. Once cooked enough, flip over
  6. Place ham and cheese once that side is cooked and fold omelette
  7. Flip again to cook evenly
