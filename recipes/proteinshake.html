Protein Shake

Description

Protein is essential for muscle growth. This shake is a fast way to get a good amount of protein in while in a time crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop of gold standard whey protein (chocolate)
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Pour milk in blender
  2. Place banana inside
  3. Put ice cubes in
  4. Add 1 scoop of protein powder
  5. Blend
